Top OTT Platforms in India

In India, OTT platforms have seen tremendous growth over the past few years and especially during the pandemic. The way people watch movies and other video content has totally changed. India is one of the fastest-growing OTT markets, with total subscriptions expected to grow from INR 19 billion in 2020 to INR 46 billion by 2022. Regional content has taken a sharper hike, and regional OTT platforms have sure swooped in and gotten quite the catch! Having said that here are the top 10 OTT platforms in India.

Netflix started offering OTT services in India in early 2016. The California-based technology company is ranked 164th among Fortune 500 companies. Netflix operates offices in many countries in Europe, Asia, and Latin America, including the United Kingdom, Canada, France, the Netherlands, Brazil, India, South Korea, and Japan. Zee5

ZEE5, one of the top OTT services in India, is owned by Zee Entertainment Enterprises. The subscription-based video-on-demand OTT streaming service was launched in February 2018 and offers content in multiple languages. The ZEE5 apps for Web, iOS, Android, and Smart TV devices are available. The home-grown OTT platform has more than 1.8 million subscribers and offers a wide range of content, including originals, TV shows, movies, and music in 12 languages. ZEE5 offers just one subscription plan of INR 999/year (currently available at INR 499 for a limited period) and supports three screens simultaneously.
